Game of Life

Game of life is a simulation of pong game and contains three complementary steps. It consists of several main parts including introduction, instruction, game story , connecting pages, steps and screens of win or lose. The main purpose of my game is, to let the player experiences different and respective stages which all human beings experience during their life in form of game and entertainment. Just like real life that we are first trained and thought how to develop our skills in order to achieve our goals, my game gives the player same opportunity to train himself through first and second steps and then, using the developed skills, succeeds to win the last level and ends up winning the game!

Real Life Game

Real life game is a simulation of Predator-prey game. The concept of this game expresses the fact that all people follow a series of common purposes in their lives which to achieve most of them, they should compete with each other. In this game, the player can be the winner in two ways. Winner is either the one who hits all the five targets or the one who hits the most targets before his competitor dies. However, getting the targets is not all they need to win. They first should stay alive and then follow the targets. To do so, every few seconds, they should overlap the family image before they lose their whole life. The other thing is that they should also be careful about the barriers. In case they go over a barrier, all targets become invisible to both of the players and to get them back, either of the players should overlap the friends picture. By making all these connections between game elements, I tried to make the players literally feel that they are in a real life situation and competition. The main template of this game was given by my professor to me and I built my own ideas upon that. The link to the main template is available here.

Chaser Game

Game - Chaser is a "simple" game of cat and mouse. The player is an owl and can move with keys, if it overlaps the (randomly moving) prey, it "eat it" by sucking out its life and adding it to its own. The player "dies" slowly over time so they have to keep eating to stay alive. This game has three levels and it gets harder as the game goes forward. The main template of this game was given by my professor to me and I built my own ideas upon that. The link to the main template is available here.
