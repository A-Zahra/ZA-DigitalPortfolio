Winner of CRAFT logo competition!

In the 2019 logo competition held by the CRAFT organizationthe most important Centre for Microfluidic Systems in Chemistry & Biology in Canada, my logo design was selected to represent the organization. CRAFT is a centre for Research and Applications in Fluidic Technologies and is operated by the Toronto University and NRC organization. I considered three indicators while designing the logo to show the features of the organization. First, I used the blue and red color to represent the Toronto University, NRC and Canada respectively. Secondly, all the letters, excluding the letter “C” are joined to figure the micro-fluidics channels that is the most important feature of my logo and shows the field of research in CRAFT. Thirdly, the maple leaf illustrates that CRAFT is a Canadian organization.
