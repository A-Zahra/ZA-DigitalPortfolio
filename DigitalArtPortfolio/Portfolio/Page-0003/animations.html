AGUMAN, THE MONSTER OF NIGHT

Aguman is a school assignment and got created in the fall 2018. The original idea of this monster belongs to some one else, but as I didn't like the initial idea, I made some changes to it, like: adding horns and ivory, stretching the eyes and reshaping the tail. Right now, Aguman is only able to fly, but I have some good ideas for it's future.
