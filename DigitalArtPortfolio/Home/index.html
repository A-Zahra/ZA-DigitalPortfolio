About me

My name is Zahra Ahmadi and I am a third-year student from Concordia University in the Computation Arts program. Over the course of three years, I was introduced to a variety of softwares and skills that broadened my knowledge in design and introduced me to the never-ending possibilities of jobs and opportunities in the design world. I have gained proficient knowledge of several design softwares such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro and Blender. Using this knowledge, I managed to participate in different logo design competitions and gain award or certificate. For instance, my design won the award of CRAFT organization logo design contest or, I got certificate for participating in EERIC organization logo design competition. I enjoy drawing and creating logos but my main interest is to to pursue my career in Animation. Feel free to contact me for any more details.
