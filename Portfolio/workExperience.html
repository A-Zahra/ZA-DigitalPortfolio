Invictus Gloves

Invictus Gloves is a Canadian company that produces its brand, and custom sports gloves for athletes, like football players. To showcase their products and the company's persona on social media, they wanted to have a mesmerizing and memorable loop video.
Being inspired by watching successful promotional videos online, I made this 3D animation loop of their custom products. I gave different colors and brands to the gloves, to show that the company produces custom gloves. Also, I used mostly black and gold colors or a mixture of both, to make this video unique for the Invictus gloves company. (the gloves' 3D model were given to me to accelerate the work in this project).
You can watch the video here, or watch it online on the Invictus Gloves Facebook page.

Winner of CRAFT logo competition!

In the 2019 logo competition held by the CRAFT organization (the most important Centre for Microfluidic Systems in Chemistry & Biology in Canada), my logo was selected to represent the organization. CRAFT is a center for Research and Applications in Fluidic Technologies and is operated by the Toronto University and NRC organization. I considered three indicators while designing the logo to show the features of the organization. First, I used blue and red colors to represent Toronto University, NRC, and Canada respectively. Second, all the letters excluding the letter “C” are joined to figure the microfluidic channels that are the most important feature of my logo and shows the field of research in CRAFT. Finally, the maple leaf illustrates that CRAFT is a Canadian organization.

Toranj

In summer 2020, a carpet weaving instructor to take the next step toward officially starting her business needed to have her own unique business card.
To make her business card represent her carrier, I decided to use carpet patterns to design both the logo and the card itself. For instance, as her brand name was "Toranj" which is inspired by a famous pattern that is usually used in persian carpets, I decided to bring that pattern into the logo design.
