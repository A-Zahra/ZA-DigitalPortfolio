Invictus Gloves

Invictus Gloves is a Canadian company that produces custom sports gloves for athletes like football players. To showcase their product, persona and Invictus colors on social media, they were looking for a mesmerizing and memorable loop video.
Being inspired by watching similar videos, I created an animation that displays all elements they asked for. For instance, by giving different colors and brands to the gloves in my video, I tried to show that the company produces custom gloves. Or, almost all colors used for the objects on the scene are black and gold or a mixture of both to make this video unique for the Invictus.
Moreover, by accomplishing this project I learned many new techniques and features of the 3D animation software I used, which makes this project an invaluable experience to me. To see the video, click on the front image.

Winner of CRAFT logo competition!

In the 2019 logo competition held by the CRAFT organization (the most important Centre for Microfluidic Systems in Chemistry & Biology in Canada), my logo design was selected to represent the organization. CRAFT is a center for Research and Applications in Fluidic Technologies and is operated by the Toronto University and NRC organization. I considered three indicators while designing the logo to show the features of the organization. First, I used blue and red colors to represent Toronto University, NRC, and Canada respectively. Secondly, all the letters excluding the letter “C” are joined to figure the microfluidic channels that are the most important feature of my logo and shows the field of research in CRAFT. Thirdly, the maple leaf illustrates that CRAFT is a Canadian organization.

Toranj

In summer 2020, a carpet weaving instructor to take the next step for officially starting her business should make her business card.
To make her business card represent her carrier, I decided to use carpet patterns to design both the logo and the card itself. For instance, as her brand name was "Toranj" which is inspired by a famous pattern that is usually used in carpet, I decided to bring that pattern into the logo design.
